🩹 Quick First Aid Tips

1

For bleeding: Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth. Do not use tourniquet.

2

For heatstroke: Move animal to shade, apply cool (not cold) water to paws and ears.

3

For poisoning: Do NOT induce vomiting. Note the substance and call the vet immediately.

4

For fractures: Keep the animal still and warm. Do not try to set the bone yourself.

5

For choking: Open the mouth carefully. If visible, try to gently remove the object.

6

Stay calm. Stressed animals respond to your energy. Speak softly and move slowly.
